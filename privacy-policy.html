Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2024

Welcome to YOURE WRONG! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: We collect your email address during registration to manage your account and app-related communication.
  • Usage Analytics: We use Vexo for analytics to monitor app usage. No analytics data is tied to your personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Email Address: Used solely for account management and communication purposes.
  • Analytics: Used to evaluate app usage against server costs and requirements. Analytics data is anonymized and not linked to personal user information.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law.

4. Retention of Data

  • Your email and any related data will be retained indefinitely.
  • Posts or situations shared in the app are kept publicly accessible for users to view and engage with; however, no personal information will ever be displayed alongside the post.

5. User Rights

  • By using YOURE WRONG, you grant us the rights to display and distribute any problems or situations you share through the app.
  • Personal Information Privacy: No personal information will be displayed or associated with your shared posts.

6. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.
